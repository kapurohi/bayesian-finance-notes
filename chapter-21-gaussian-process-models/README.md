# Chapter 21 — Gaussian Process Models

A Gaussian process (GP) is a prior over functions. Instead of specifying a parametric functional form and estimating its parameters, a GP places a prior directly over the space of all possible smooth functions. It is the nonparametric Bayesian approach to regression and classification.
