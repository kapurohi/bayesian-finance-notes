# Chapter 19 — Parametric Nonlinear Models

Linear models are convenient but often wrong. Many financial relationships are inherently nonlinear — diminishing returns, threshold effects, exponential growth. This chapter covers Bayesian nonlinear regression where the relationship between predictors and outcome is a known nonlinear function.
