# Chapter 13 — Modal and Distributional Approximations

When exact MCMC is too slow and variational inference is too inaccurate, a middle ground exists: approximations that are more principled than VI but faster than full MCMC. This chapter covers Laplace approximation, the Laplace approximation for hierarchical models, and Integrated Nested Laplace Approximation (INLA).
